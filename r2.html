Custom R2 Unit

  • Autodesk Maya
  • Substance Painter

  After watching Star Wars again, I wanted to create a custom R2 unit that I thought I would use if I were in the universe! So I decided to do a 3D project, creating a model based off of R2D2 then textured and slightly adjusted to fit my style. This was overall supposed to be a creative project, with a timeline restriction of 15 hours.

  Because this was a cylindrical model with a spherical top, it was a bit of a challenge for myself. Creating the details and shape was a challenge due to small details and being limited to the basic tools that Autodesk Maya offered. I went through three iterations due to multiple bugs, but debugging the software and issues of my own was a part of the learning process I wished to use during the creation of this piece.

  I modelled this R2 unit in Autodesk maya, initially creating a low poly model (which can be seen in the untextured model). I then increased the detail and polycount and textured it in Substance painter. I enjoyed this process as it was something new and a challenge as well.

Timeline: 15 hours
Prior Experience: Basic 3D modelling experience, Two time experience in UV mapping, texturing, and rendering
Project Purpose: Personal Study
